Connectivity explosion

We are seeing an explosion of Integration and connectivity due to various factors. The integration technolgies and approaches we had 10 years ago won't fit in this new landscape.

Integration v1.0

  • Enterprise Service Bus
  • SOA + Web Services
  • Enterprise Integration patterns
The earlier round of Integration did have challenges to solve, but was trying to solve the problems present at the time such as how to connect Databases, Mainframes, ERP systems, File transfer. Not typically direct to the public, focussed on internal & B2B transactions. We are trying to achieve a lot of the same objectives the original goals like -- loose coupling, event driven systems but now in a different way than buying a Vendor's product and the options for connecting outside the enterprise have also exploded. (PAAS, SAAS).

Integration v2.0

  • Internet of Things
  • Mobiles, Digital channels
  • Platform / Software as a service
  • API's
  • Containerisation
So integration 2.0 has to evolve to take into account all of these developments, all of them have contributed to an increase in connectivity and the introduction of the 'API / Platform economy' where companies have changed or disrupted their markets based on the success of their Platform, API's & Digital products.

Public API Growth

In a few short years we've seen an explosion in public API growth, finance and insurance is as typically a conservative follower of technology but we are seeing the startups in movements like Fintech challenge traditional players in our markets.
